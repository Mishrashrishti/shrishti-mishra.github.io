About Me

My research interests lie in Large Language Models (LLMs) with a focus on multilingual and low-resource NLP[cite: 2]. I aim to address challenges in data scarcity and quality to democratize AI for global communities speaking underrepresented languages[cite: 2, 4].

Currently, I am working on Project EKA, developing synthetic data generation pipelines for educational content[cite: 12, 13]. Previously, I built an Indic Malayalam Language Model from scratch, handling everything from curation of 120GB of data to Llama-based architecture training[cite: 9, 10, 11].

Beyond core NLP, I have experience in Reinforcement Learning for musculoskeletal simulations and Human Activity Recognition[cite: 7, 8, 73].
